Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

How do I check my gift card balance?
You can check your gift card balance online, through the Starbucks app, at any Starbucks store, or by calling 1-800-STARBUC.
How do I reload my Starbucks Card?
Reload your card through the Starbucks app, online at Starbucks.com, or at any Starbucks store register.
What is Starbucks' return policy?
We offer refunds for Starbucks Card purchases and merchandise returns within 90 days with receipt. Food and beverage items cannot be returned.
How do I apply for a job at Starbucks?
Visit our Opportunities page to view current openings and submit your application online.
Does Starbucks offer catering?
Yes! Many stores offer catering services. Contact your local store or visit our catering page for more information.
How do I report an issue with a store?
Please use our contact form above or call customer service. Provide as much detail as possible including store location and date/time of visit.
