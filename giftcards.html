Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I use Starbucks Gift Cards?
Starbucks Gift Cards can be used at participating Starbucks stores in the U.S. and Canada, on Starbucks.com, in the Starbucks app, and at Starbucks Store locations.
Do Starbucks Gift Cards expire?
No, Starbucks Gift Cards do not expire. There are also no fees for non-use.
Can I reload my Starbucks Gift Card?
Yes, you can reload your Starbucks Gift Card at any Starbucks store, online at Starbucks.com, or through the Starbucks app.
How do I check my gift card balance?
You can check your balance at any Starbucks store, online using the form above, through the Starbucks app, or by calling 1-800-STARBUC.
